# Built-In Playbooks

## BOSS Resume Screening

Use visible, authorized candidate information only. Require a frozen job scorecard, evidence-backed job-related scoring, masked identifiers, minimized stored fields, and a human review queue. Under the weak safety gate, allow task-authorized neutral messages plus archive, block, report, follow, routine permission, and non-security profile actions without per-action confirmation. Never make a final hiring decision, candidate rejection, interview invitation, security/ownership change, payment/purchase/refund/order, login, captcha/2FA, privileged device change, or bulk export.

## Acquisition

Use this acquisition workflow for public or user-authorized visible discovery, demand-signal classification, lead summaries, and outreach content. Run automatic outbound for publish, comment, private-message, follow, and add-contact steps when the normalized task provides task-level authorization and preflight passes. Enforce target scope, content policy, frequency cap, duplicate prevention, audit log, and per-action evidence. Do not perform untargeted unsolicited bulk outreach, scrape hidden APIs, evade platform controls, or store credentials, private contact details, or unneeded personal data.

Both playbooks require selected-device evidence, task IDs where available, visible proof, and a concrete human next action. Promote a recipe only after the route meets the verified-recipe rule.
